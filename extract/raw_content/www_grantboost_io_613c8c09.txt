**Ethical Considerations in Grant Writing Fees**
------------------------------------------------

Understanding these principles is essential for establishing appropriate fee structures and maintaining credibility with funding sources.

### **Commission-Based Fees (Percentage of Award)**

While some grant writers offer commission-based fee structures, this practice raises significant ethical concerns.

**Industry standard**: Considered unethical by professional associations

* Grant Professionals Association (GPA) prohibits this practice
* Association of Fundraising Professionals (AFP) discourages it
* Many foundations explicitly forbid it in grant terms

**Typical (but discouraged) rates**: 2-5% of the grant award

**Risks**:

* Potential violation of grant terms
* Misaligned incentives
* Damage to organizational reputation
* Possible disqualification from funding
